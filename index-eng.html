We work since 2003

Oberig-IF Charitable Foundation was founded in 2003 to help vulnerable groups, support culture and sports.

Since the beginning of the 2014 war, we have been actively involved in helping the military at the front. Our team helped the military, their families, hospitals and migrants.

Over the years, we have established friendly cooperation with volunteer organizations and caring people from around the world. It is a resource that helps us respond quickly to requests and needs.

With the beginning of the full-scale Russian invasion of our land, we are again in the volunteer ranks.

Necessary ammunition for the military, optics, communications, tactical medicine and basic necessities. Food, clothing and personal hygiene products for the civilian population are relevant.

Areas of work:

  • Targeted assistance to the military, units
  • Purchase of optics, thermal imagers and more
  • Assistance to hospitals, hospitals
  • Assistance to migrants
  • Assistance to the civilian population in the combat zone
  • Cooperation with volunteer organizations of the city, region, country
